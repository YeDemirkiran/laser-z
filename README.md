# Lazer Z: A mobile hypercasual game
Lazer Z is a hypercasual mobile game, set in an apocalyptic world full of rotten zombies and other dangerous creatures.

[(Türkçe README için tıklayınız.)](README_TR.md)

## Gameplay
### Objective
Your main objective is to survive as much as you can against zombie hordes coming towards you, and get the best score. 

Your score increases over time. Additionally, you get extra score points for each zombie you kill.

### Mechanics
- Controls: Simply swipe left and right on your phone to move your character. It will automaticaly fire at the enemy coming towards you.
- Collectables: Shoot the collectables on your way to gain health points, upgrade your gun and unlock new guns.

### Weapons
- Turret Gun: Double-firing gun. The first weapon you unlock automatically.
- Minigun: The second gun you unlock. Very high fire rate.
- Shotgun: The third gun. Fires 5 bullets simultaneously, and it is your duty to not let them go to waste.
- Laser Gun: The fourth and final gun. Slower fire rate but the plasma goes through all targets, zombies and collectables, giving you the opportunity to take multiple targets at once with a well-timed shot.
